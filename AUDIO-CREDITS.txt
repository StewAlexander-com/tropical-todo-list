Quiet — asset credits
=====================

Cinematic beach video (assets/beach-*.mp4, assets/beach-poster*.jpg)
--------------------------------------------------------------------
AI-generated with Veo (image-to-video) from the repository owner's own beach
photograph as the starting frame. Day and dusk color grades produced with ffmpeg.
The seamless loop is achieved at runtime via a dual-video crossfade (rain-view
pattern). Ensure you hold rights to the source photo before publishing.

Ocean waves ambience (assets/waves.mp3)
----------------------------------------
Procedurally synthesized in this repository (see _build/make_waves.py). CC0.

Pattern credit
--------------
The dual-video crossfade loop and audio unlock / start-muted / visibility-resume
patterns are adapted from the author's own projects rain-view and pocket-card
(github.com/StewAlexander-com).
