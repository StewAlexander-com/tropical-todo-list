Quiet — audio credits
=====================

Ocean waves ambience (assets/waves.mp3)
----------------------------------------
Procedurally synthesized in this repository (see _build/make_waves.py) — layered
filtered noise shaped by a slow swell envelope, crossfaded into a seamless ~23s
loop. Original work, no third-party samples. Free to reuse (CC0).

Animation pattern
-----------------
The audio unlock + start-muted + visibility-resume pattern is adapted from the
author's own Pocket Card project (github.com/StewAlexander-com/pocket-card),
itself derived from the Rain View pattern.
